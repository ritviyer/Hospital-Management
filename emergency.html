Overview

Emergency medicine is a specialty that is concerned with the initial assessment, stabilization, diagnosis and treatment of individuals with acute illness and injury. It also offers management of trauma and resuscitation, advanced cardiac life support and airway management, poisoning, overdose, out-of-hospital care and disaster casualties. It also involves the technical and cognitive aspects of practically all fields of medicine and surgery including the surgical sub-specialties. The Emergency Medical Services of the hospital is the community's main access to the hospital in an emergency and is also the most visible part of the emergency department to the public eye.
