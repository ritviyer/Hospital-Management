Overview

The Department of Orthopaedics caters to a variety of Orthopedic problems as well as Trauma services. We have a competent Trauma Team handling all types of trauma including complex pelvic and acetabular injuries. We also have specialist surgeons performing Spine surgeries and Endoscopic Spine surgeries, Arthroscopy and Sports medicine including Arthroscopic hip, knee and shoulder surgeries, Joint Replacement Surgeries, Paediatric Orthopaedic surgeries and Deformity Correction.

Orthopaedics sub-specialization allows intense focus of our Faculty in the areas of Hand, Upper Extremity & Microsurgery, Paediatric orthopaedics, Spine, Reconstructive Orthopaedics, Arthroscopy and sports injuries, joint replacement and Trauma providing outstanding treatment to all.

Key Features

  • Department covers 24 hours trauma , Emergency care
  • Manages all types of Musculoskeletal injuries including Spine
  • Facilities for Limb reconstruction and joint replacement
  • Facilities for Spine surgeries
  • Facilities for deformity correction and treatment for chronic osteomyelitis with ILIZAROV technique

Core Competencies

  • Hand, wrist, elbow and micro surgery
  • Paediatric Orthopaedics
  • Spine Surgery
  • Sports Medicine
