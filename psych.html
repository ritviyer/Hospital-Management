Overview

The department of Psychiatry is actively involved in clinical work, teaching and research activities. The facilities available are a comprehensive out-patient and in-patient mental healthcare services. The assessment and treatment of psychiatric disorders is carried out by an interdisciplinary team of Psychiatrists, Psychologists, Psychiatric Social Workers and Psychiatric Nurses. The patients are managed using both pharmacotherapy, physical therapies (Electro-convulsive therapy, rtMS etc) & psychotherapy.

The Psychiatry Department at RM Hospital is dedicated to building and providing better, evidence-driven models of clinical care for conditions affecting individuals across the age spectrum with severe and diverse illnesses.

Key Features

  • The Department of Psychiatry offers comprehensive, innovative and recovery-focused services to help with mental health problems
  • Child and Adolescent Psychiatry and Child Development
  • General Psychiatry and Psychology
  • Interdisciplinary Brain Sciences
  • Public Mental Health and Population Sciences
  • Conduct quality and ethical research for the growth of scientific knowledge and its application
  • The Department also handles common mental disorders like anxiety, obsessive compulsive disorders (OCD) and depression, schizophrenia, mood disorders and dementia or substance abuse disorders like alcohol abuse and personality disorders
